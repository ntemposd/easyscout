Privacy Policy

Easyscout Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2026

Easyscout ("we", "us") provides AI-assisted basketball scouting reports. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and your choices.

Data we collect

  • Account data: email, name (if provided), and authentication tokens from Supabase.
  • Usage data: page views, feature interactions, device and browser info for analytics.
  • Payment data: processed by Stripe; we do not store full card details.
  • Content you submit: player names, queries, and generated reports.

How we use data

  • Provide and improve the scouting service.
  • Billing, fraud prevention, and support.
  • Analytics to understand product usage and reliability.
  • Security, abuse prevention, and legal compliance.

Cookies and local storage

  • Essential: session/auth tokens to keep you signed in.
  • Analytics: PostHog for product analytics and event tracking.
  • Preferences: banner acknowledgements and UI settings.

You can block cookies in your browser, but some features may not work.

Third parties

  • Supabase (authentication, database, storage).
  • PostHog (product analytics).
  • Stripe (payments).

Data retention

  • Account and billing records: kept while your account is active and as required by law.
  • Analytics data: retained per our analytics configuration; aggregates may be pruned.
  • Reports and inputs: retained to operate the service unless you request deletion.

Your choices

  • Opt out of non-essential cookies via your browser settings.
  • Access, update, or delete your account data by contacting support.
  • Request export or deletion of your reports and personal data (subject to legal obligations).

Security

We use HTTPS, Supabase access controls, and delegate payment security to Stripe (PCI-compliant).

International transfers

Data may be processed in the US or EU depending on provider locations (Supabase, PostHog, Stripe).

Children

Not directed to children under 16; do not use the service if you are under 16.

Contact

Email: support@easyscout.com

Updates

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date reflects changes.
